About Me

Originally from New York, Emily is a candidate for a Bachelor of Science in Architecture at Northeastern University. For the past year Emily has been intensely studying famous architectural landmarks and creating original designs ranging from public pavilions to private residences. Emily also has a background in fine art and design, bringing a unique visionary aspect to her work. In the future, Emily hopes to travel the world, continue her studies, and work within the architectural field.
